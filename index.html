Our Story

Founded in 2025, KenEi Solutions emerged from a clear need to help organizations simplify complex information and transform it into powerful, actionable solutions.

“We exist to make innovation accessible – distilling technology into its most impactful form for real-world change.”

At KenEi Solutions, our mission is to harness the power of technology and AI to tackle Africa’s most pressing challenges. From healthcare and housing to finance and education, we’re committed to developing innovative, real-world solutions that bring tangible change to underserved communities.

Our journey began with a bold belief: that technology can bridge gaps and create opportunity where it’s needed most.
